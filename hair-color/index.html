good to know

Color FAQs

Can I go blonde in one appointment?
It depends on your starting point and hair history. Healthy hair comes first — sometimes the best result takes two sessions, and I'll tell you upfront if that's the case.
I dyed my hair at home and it went wrong. Can you fix it?
This is genuinely one of my specialties. Book a consultation (and be honest about everything that's been on your hair!) and I'll build a correction plan.
How often will I need touch-ups?
Lived-in techniques can stretch 3+ months between visits; vivids and rooted colors vary. We'll pick a technique that matches how often you actually want to be in the salon.
How should I prep for a color appointment?
Come with hair that's been washed in the last day or two, wear a shirt you don't mind risking, and bring your inspiration photos. That's it.
