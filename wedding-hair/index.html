Wedding FAQs

How far in advance should I book?
As soon as you have your date and venue. Popular months go quickly — six months out is comfortable, more is better.
Do you travel to the venue?
Yes — on-location styling is the whole point. I come to your venue, hotel, or getting-ready spot in the Omaha area so your morning stays relaxed.
Can you do my whole bridal party?
Absolutely. Share your headcount and your photographer's timeline and I'll map out the morning so everyone's finished with time to spare.
What about extensions or a trial color before the wedding?
Great instinct — extensions add fullness that photographs beautifully, and color should be done 1–2 weeks before the day. We can plan all of it in one consultation.
