Hair Extensions in Omaha

If you've been growing your hair out forever, fighting thin ends, or just want that full, effortless look — extensions are the shortcut. I offer beaded weft, tape-in, and K-tip extensions plus mesh integration systems, blended and matched so no one can tell where your hair ends.

Extensions that look like they grew there

The difference between good and bad extensions is everything: placement, color match, and blending. I take the time to custom-match your color and cut the extensions into your hair so the result moves and lives like your own.

Beaded wefts: Rows of weft hair secured with small beads — no glue, no heat, and lots of fullness. Tape-ins: Thin, flexible panels that lie flat and blend beautifully in finer hair. K-tips: Small keratin-bonded strands for the most customizable, undetectable placement.

Mesh integration: A breathable mesh system your natural hair is integrated through — a game-changer for fine or thinning hair, giving coverage and volume without stressing your own strands. Which method fits you best is exactly what we figure out at your consultation.

Worried about damage? Healthy installation and proper maintenance are non-negotiable for me. I'll be honest with you about what your hair can handle.

Extension FAQs

How much do extensions cost?
It depends on the method, the amount of hair, and your goals — which is why I quote at the consultation instead of guessing. The consultation is where you get a real number for your exact hair.
What is mesh integration?
A lightweight, breathable mesh base sits at the scalp and your natural hair is pulled through and blended with extension hair. It's ideal if your hair is fine or thinning, or you want serious volume and coverage — without heavy wefts pulling on fragile strands.
Will extensions damage my hair?
Not when they're installed and maintained properly. Damage comes from poor placement, skipped maintenance, or the wrong method for your hair type. I'll always tell you honestly what your hair can handle.
How long do they last?
The hair itself typically lasts months with good care. You'll come in periodically for move-up appointments as your natural hair grows — we'll set that schedule together.
Can I still wear my hair up?
Yes! Proper placement means ponytails and braids are absolutely doable. I place everything with your real life in mind.
