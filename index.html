About Me

Hey, I'm Syd! I got into this industry because I love helping people feel confident and beautiful. There's nothing better than seeing someone light up when they look in the mirror and love what they see.

I work out of a private salon suite in West Omaha, which means your appointment is just you and me — no crowded salon floor, no rushing. I'm always learning and growing so I can give you the best experience possible, whether you're here for extensions, a fresh color, or styling for your big day.
